ROBLOX Present

The game section

The roblox game section is filled to the brim with all types of games and genres. Some of these being Action, Adventure, Education, Obby & Platformer, Party & Casual, Puzzle, Roleplay, and Survival. Most if not all of these games come from the community. This is because Roblox is a app with user generated content including the games as mentioned.

The community

The community is a place where all the players get together on Roblox to create, explore and socialize with one another. It is also a place where you can show your creativity, true passion and hobbies. It is also a very inclusive place for all types of people most the time.

The avatars

An avatar is an outfit that you wear to express your true self or your looks. With this comes an entry to all types of avatars people wear. There are many different forms of avatars including Meme avatars, Animal avatars, Aesthetic avatars, and Modern avatars. This is because Roblox is a market place with user made items from the community of Roblox. This can reflect on the new trends you see people wearing each week, because of the new items made by users that begin to trend.
