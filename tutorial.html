ROBLOX Tutorial

How to play

Firstly you need any device that has Roblox installed. You can get it from the Roblox website. After that you can just learn the controls based on the device you play on. If you still struggle to learn the basics you can always look up tutorials online, or just ask your friends or community for help.

How to stay safe

When you or anybody else is on Roblox, it is always important to know that not everyone you meet online is really who you think they are. The truth is that you should always be careful with whoever you're talking to online. The best way to stay safe is by never ever giving out any type of personal information that links back to you, like your phone number, name, address, or anything personal about you.

How to enjoy the game

Now that you're done with that, all you really need to do is play the variety of games that Roblox has to offer. Doing this better helps you find what types of games connect to you best as a person. Roblox has a varitiy of options to exlpore such as roleplaying, obbys, first shooters, simulation, and trends which you certainly will notice when you see the front page for the first time.
